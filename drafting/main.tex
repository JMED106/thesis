%%%%%%%%%% Motivation of this work, key points: obtain better
%%%%%%%%%% understanding of how the brain works. We want to discover
%%%%%%%%%% the fundamental laws which shape the dynamics of the
%%%%%%%%%% brain. In a similar way as it is done when studying the
%%%%%%%%%% laws of thermodynamics and statistical mechanics. How the
%%%%%%%%%% microscopic dynamics of single neurons interacting in large
%%%%%%%%%% population networks determine or shape the macroscopic
%%%%%%%%%% dynamics of the population. What are the fundamental
%%%%%%%%%% properties of their interactions and how these laws may
%%%%%%%%%% radically change their collective dynamics. 

%%%%%%%%%% Conclusions or major findings: correlations between neuron
%%%%%%%%%% dynamics, in terms of spike correlations, is a fundamental
%%%%%%%%%% aspect of microscopic dynamics which radically changes the
%%%%%%%%%% macroscopic behavior of their collective interactions. We
%%%%%%%%%% have presented a new macroscopic model of neural dynamics
%%%%%%%%%% which for the first time accounts for spike
%%%%%%%%%% synchronization. We show that in addition to the already
%%%%%%%%%% known (studied) macroscopic phenomena, the model displays a
%%%%%%%%%% grate variety of novel macroscopic dynamics which can be
%%%%%%%%%% directly related to the microscopic biological parameters
%%%%%%%%%% determining the dynamics of single neurons, and also to the
%%%%%%%%%% interactions between the neurons.
